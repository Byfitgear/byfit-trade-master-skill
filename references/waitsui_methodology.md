## Module 1: 外贸人基本素养

### 1.1 现实自我评估

外土司强调：做外贸之前，先认清自己。不是每个人都适合做外贸，也不是每个外贸人都能做好。

**三问自测**：

| 问题 | 合格标准 | 不合格信号 |
|------|----------|------------|
| 你的英语够用吗？ | 能写商务邮件、能电话沟通、能看懂客户网站的行业术语 | 依赖翻译软件写邮件，电话完全听不懂 |
| 你的产品够熟吗？ | 能脱稿介绍产品、能回答80%的技术问题、能指出竞品差异 | 客户一问细节就要去问技术，回复慢 |
| 你的心态够稳吗？ | 能接受3个月没有订单、能面对客户的沉默和拒绝 | 一周不回就焦虑，被拒绝就丧失信心 |

### 1.2 英语要求 — 够用就好

外土司的观点：**外贸英语不需要"英语好"，需要"英语够用"**。

**够用的标准**：
- 邮件写作：能用简洁的商务英语表达观点，不追求华丽，追求清晰
- 电话沟通：能听懂对方的核心诉求，能说出关键信息
- 行业术语：熟悉你产品的英文专业词汇

**不够用的信号**：
- 写一封邮件要查30分钟词典
- 每句话都用Google翻译再"精修"
- 电话沟通完全依赖文字聊天

**提升方法**：
- 每天读5封客户邮件，积累表达方式
- 建立自己的邮件模板库，不是复制模板，而是收集好用的表达
- 录音回听自己的电话，找改进点

### 1.3 工作方法 — 效率与深度

**外土司工作四原则**：

1. **固定节奏** — 每天固定时间处理邮件（上午9-10点，下午4-5点），其余时间做深度工作
2. **分类处理** — 邮件按紧急/重要四象限分类，不要"先进先出"
3. **一次性处理** — 打开一封邮件就处理完，不要反复打开
4. **记录归档** — 每个客户的沟通要点、需求、痛点都要记录，不靠记忆靠系统

**BYFIT 应用**：

作为BYFIT的业务员，你同时面对gym flooring、gym equipment、pilates equipment三条产品线，客户类型也不同（分销商、健身房、品牌商）。必须有清晰的工作系统：

```
每日工作节奏：
09:00-10:00  处理 overnight 邮件（欧美客户工作时间）
10:00-12:00  主动开发（Google搜索、LinkedIn联系）
14:00-15:00  处理当日新邮件（亚洲/中东客户）
15:00-17:00  跟进谈判（报价、方案、逼单）
17:00-18:00  记录归档、准备次日工作
```

## Module 2: 询盘邮件（Inquiry & Email）

### 2.1 网红式标题 — 让客户打开你的邮件

外土司的核心观点：**标题是邮件的"门面"，门面不好，内容再好也没人看**。

#### 标题四型公式

**1. 数字型 (Numbers)**
- 人类大脑对数字天然敏感
- 数字给邮件一种"量化、可信"的感觉

```
❌ Rubber flooring for your gym
✅ 3 reasons gyms switch to BYFIT rubber flooring
✅ 15mm rubber tiles: 40% better impact absorption than 10mm
✅ 2,000+ gyms use BYFIT flooring — here's why
```

**2. 提问型 (Questions)**
- 提问激发好奇心
- 问题要切中客户可能关心的话题

```
❌ BYFIT gym equipment catalog
✅ Looking for a reliable gym equipment supplier for 2025?
✅ Is your rubber flooring supplier causing returns?
✅ Why do 3 of the top 10 gym distributors in [Country] source from BYFIT?
```

**3. 好奇型 (Curiosity)**
- 不透露全部信息，让对方想打开看
- 关键是"留悬念"

```
❌ Our new pilates reformer
✅ The pilates equipment most studios don't know about yet
✅ Something different in rubber flooring — you'll want to see this
✅ A flooring spec that reduces gym noise by 50%
```

**4. 直接型 (Direct)**
- 适合已经有一定联系的客户
- 直接、明确、不废话

```
❌ Hello from BYFIT
✅ BYFIT rubber flooring quotation — [Company Name]
✅ Re: Your inquiry for pilates reformers — pricing enclosed
✅ Sample shipment confirmed — BYFIT gym flooring
```

#### BYFIT 标题选择指南

| 场景 | 推荐标题类型 | 示例 |
|------|-------------|------|
| 首次联系新客户 | 好奇型/提问型 | "Is your gym flooring supplier causing returns?" |
| 回复询盘 | 直接型 | "Re: Your inquiry for rubber flooring — BYFIT" |
| 跟进沉默客户 | 数字型 | "3 ways BYFIT reduces your flooring cost" |
| 逼单 | 直接型 | "Production slot reserved — BYFIT order confirmation" |

### 2.2 询盘深度分析法（三步法）

外土司的询盘分析不是"看一眼就回"，而是先分析后回复。

**Step 1: 身份识别 (Identity)**

从询盘中判断对方是谁：

| 信号 | 身份判断 | BYFIT 应对方向 |
|------|----------|----------------|
| 公司名包含"Distribution"/"Supply" | 分销商 | 批量价格+区域保护 |
| 公司名包含"Fitness"/"Gym"/"Studio" | 终端用户 | 零售指引+推荐当地分销商 |
| 公司名包含"Brand"或有®标志 | 品牌商 | OEM/ODM方案+MOQ |
| 免费邮箱(gmail/hotmail) | 小客户或中间人 | 先确认身份再投入精力 |
| 企业邮箱+专业签名 | 正规公司 | 高优先级回复 |
| 询盘内容非常专业详细 | 内行买家 | 专业回复，不废话 |
| 询盘内容模糊("send me catalog") | 可能是钓鱼或新手 | 简短回复+确认需求 |

**Step 2: 意图判断 (Intent)**

| 意图类型 | 特征 | 判断方法 |
|----------|------|----------|
| 真实采购 | 产品描述具体、数量明确、有交期要求 | 直接报价 |
| 比价 | 只问价格不给数量、同时向多家询价 | 报价但加入价值语境 |
| 信息收集 | 问很多技术问题但不提采购 | 提供有限信息，换取对方更多信息 |
| 骗取方案 | 要详细方案但从不推进 | 给框架方案，关键细节留到确认身份后 |

**Step 3: 等级评定 (Grade)**

| 等级 | 标准 | 投入精力 | 响应时间 |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| A级 | 真实采购+明确需求+正规公司 | 高 | 2小时内 |
| B级 | 有采购意向但需求不明确 | 中 | 4小时内 |
| C级 | 意图不明/小客户 | 低 | 24小时内 |
| D级 | 疑似钓鱼/无价值 | 极低 | 可不回或模板回 |

**BYFIT 询盘分析示例**：

```
询盘原文：
"We are looking for rubber flooring for a gym project in Dubai.
Need approximately 500 sqm, 15mm thickness, black color.
Please send your best price CIF Jebel Ali."

分析：
- Identity: 迪拜买家（终端/项目方），企业邮箱
- Intent: 真实采购（具体产品、数量、港口）
- Grade: A级（数量可观、需求明确、有具体项目）
- 回复策略：2小时内回复，CIF报价，突出项目经验
```

### 2.3 询盘回复差异化策略

外土司强调：**99%的外贸人用同样的方式回复询盘——报价单+catalog。你要做那1%**。

**差异化回复三原则**：

1. **先确认再报价** — 不急于发报价单，先确认需求（见毅冰Q1策略）
2. **带价值语境** — 报价不只是数字，告诉对方"为什么值这个价"
3. **主动建议** — 不要被动等客户指示，主动给出专业建议

**BYFIT 差异化回复示例**：

普通回复（99%的人这样写）：
```
Dear Sir,

Thanks for your inquiry. Please find attached our quotation and
catalog. Looking forward to your reply.

Best regards,
```

差异化回复（1%的人这样写）：
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for your inquiry about rubber flooring for your Dubai project.

A few observations based on your specs:

1. 15mm thickness is perfect for free weight areas. If any part of
   your gym is for cardio/stretching, 8mm would be sufficient and
   save you about 30% on that area.

2. For Dubai's climate, I recommend our HeatShield formulation
   (SBR + 30% EPDM) — it handles 50°C+ ambient temperature and
   has UV resistance for any outdoor sections.

3. CIF Jebel Ali is no problem. I can also arrange door delivery
   to your project site if needed.

Let me prepare a detailed quotation. Could you confirm:
- Is this for one gym or multiple locations?
- Any color preferences beyond black?
- Installation by your team or do you need adhesive/accessories?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**差异化要点**：
- 第1点：主动帮客户省钱（不同区域用不同厚度）—— 专业 + 贴心
- 第2点：根据对方环境给专业建议 —— 不是在卖产品，是在解决问题
- 第3点：提供超出期望的服务选项
- 最后的确认问题：深入了解需求 + 创造互动机会

### 2.4 邮件防守技巧

外土司的"邮件防守"是指在不利情况下如何保护自己的利益。

**常见防守场景**：

**场景1：客户说"别人价格更低"**

```
❌ "We can match that price." （自降身价）
❌ "Then you should buy from them." （赌气）

✅ "I understand price is important. Could you share what spec they're
   quoting? In rubber flooring, a $2/sqm difference usually means a
   different material grade. I'd be happy to do an apples-to-apples
   comparison so you can make an informed decision."
```

**场景2：客户要你免费寄大量样品**

```
❌ "We don't provide free samples." （直接拒绝）
❌ "Sure, I'll send everything for free." （没有原则）

✅ "We'd love to support your evaluation. Our standard sample program:
   - Up to 5 tile samples (mixed specs/colors): Free of charge
   - Full sample kit (10+ items): $XXX (fully refundable against
     first order over $X,XXX)
   This ensures we're both serious about the partnership."
```

**场景3：客户要求超长账期**

```
❌ "We don't offer credit terms." （死板）
❌ "OK, 90 days is fine." （风险太大）

✅ "For new partnerships, our standard is 30% deposit + 70% against
   B/L copy. After 3 successful orders with on-time payments, we can
   discuss extended terms. This protects both sides during the
   trust-building phase."
```

### 2.5 关键词收集法

外土司强调：**每个行业都有客户常用的"关键词"，收集并使用这些关键词，能让你的邮件更专业、更可信**。

**BYFIT 关键词库（部分）**：

| 产品线 | 技术关键词 | 应用关键词 | 商业关键词 |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Rubber Flooring | SBR, EPDM, vulcanization, density, durometer (Shore A), critical fall height, EN14904, fire rating (Bfl-s1) | free weight area, cardio zone, crossfit box, outdoor gym, playground | FOB, CIF, MOQ, container load, exclusive territory, warranty |
| Gym Equipment | powder coating, steel gauge (3mm/2.5mm), cable rating, weight stack, biomechanics | commercial gym, home gym, hotel fitness, corporate wellness | OEM, ODM, private label, assembly, warranty |
| Pilates Equipment | reformer, cadillac, spring resistance, carriage, footbar, rope system | pilates studio, rehab center, boutique fitness | studio package, instructor discount, certification partnership |

**使用原则**：
- 不要堆砌关键词——用得自然，用得精准
- 在标题中使用1个关键词，正文中使用2-3个
- 确保你真正理解每个关键词的含义——用错了比不用更糟

## Module 3: 客户管理（Client Management）

### 3.1 客户分类与管理系统

外土司的客户分类不是简单的A/B/C，而是多维度评估。

**客户价值评估矩阵**：

| 维度 | 权重 | 评估标准 |
|------|------|----------|
| 订单量 | 30% | 年采购金额 / 潜在采购金额 |
| 利润率 | 25% | 毛利率水平（高利润客户 > 低利润大客户） |
| 成长性 | 20% | 市场增长 + 客户自身增长潜力 |
| 合作难度 | 15% | 沟通成本、定制要求、付款条件 |
| 忠诚度 | 10% | 转换供应商的可能性 |

**BYFIT 客户分类示例**：

| 客户类型 | 特征 | BYFIT 对应产品 | 管理策略 |
|----------|------|---------------|----------|
| 大型分销商 | 年采购 > $500K，多SKU | 三条产品线全覆盖 | 专属客户经理，季度回顾，市场保护 |
| 区域代理 | 覆盖1-2个国家，中等规模 | Flooring + Equipment | 区域保护，联合营销 |
| 项目方 | 单次项目采购，不稳定 | 视项目而定 | 项目制跟进，不投入过多维护成本 |
| 品牌商 | OEM/ODM需求，高定制 | Equipment + Pilates | 技术团队对接，NDA签署 |
| 小型工作室 | 采购量小，但可能成长 | Pilates equipment | 标准化服务，低维护成本 |

### 3.2 客户价值过滤 — 不是所有人都值得当客户

外土司的名言：**"不是所有人都值得当你的客户"**。

**应该主动放弃的客户**：

1. **价格屠夫型** — 只看价格，每年砍价20%，不断更换供应商
2. **拖欠货款型** — 付款习惯差，应收账款超过90天
3. **要求无底线型** — 免费样品、免费设计、免费仓储、免费物流，但没有回馈
4. **精神消耗型** — 沟通成本极高，反复修改需求，确认后推翻重来

**BYFIT 过滤话术**：

```
"Thank you for your interest in BYFIT. Based on your requirements,
I think a recycled-rubber flooring supplier might be a better fit
for your price point. Our focus is on virgin SBR and EPDM products
with certified quality. I'm happy to recommend some alternatives
if that would help."
```

**注意**：不是拒绝，而是帮对方找到更合适的选择——这是专业和尊重的表现。

### 3.3 潜在订单挖掘

**五条挖掘路径**：

1. **交叉销售** — 买flooring的客户，可能也需要gym equipment
   - "Since you're setting up a gym, would you also like to see our equipment line?"

2. **项目延伸** — 一个项目完成后，可能有第二期
   - "Congratulations on completing your first gym! Are there plans for a second location?"

3. **市场拓展** — 客户在一个市场成功后，可能想进入相邻市场
   - "Your gym flooring business is strong in [Country]. Have you considered expanding to [neighboring country]?"

4. **季节性机会** — New Year（健身高峰期）前6个月是采购高峰
   - "January is peak season for gym openings. If you want to capture that demand, now is the time to order."

5. **转介绍** — 满意客户是最好的营销
   - "If you know other gym equipment distributors who might benefit from our products, I'd appreciate an introduction."

### 3.4 客户送礼策略（分层制）

外土司的送礼原则：**送礼不是贿赂，是维护关系的仪式感。但必须有策略，不能乱送**。

**分层送礼方案**：

| 客户等级 | 送礼时机 | 礼物类型 | 预算 |
|----------|----------|----------|------|
| A级（Top 20%） | 圣诞节 + 春节 | 定制礼物（带客户logo或中国文化元素） | $100-200 |
| B级（Top 50%） | 圣诞节 | 实用商务礼物（笔、笔记本、茶叶） | $30-50 |
| C级（其余） | 圣诞节 | 电子贺卡 | $0 |
| 新客户（首次下单） | 订单确认后 | 中国特色小礼物随货发出 | $10-20 |

**BYFIT 送礼建议**：

- **A级客户**：定制BYFIT + 客户联名健身包（实用 + 品牌曝光），附手写贺卡
- **B级客户**：中国龙井茶礼盒 + BYFIT品牌运动水壶
- **C级客户**：精心设计的电子圣诞贺卡（包含BYFIT年度新品预览）
- **注意**：中东客户注意Halal合规，不送含酒精的礼物；了解客户国家的送礼法规

**送礼邮件模板**：

```
Subject: A small token from BYFIT this holiday season

Hi [Name],

As the year comes to a close, I want to thank you for being a
valued partner. Working with [Company Name] has been a highlight
of our year.

A small gift is on its way — nothing fancy, just something to
show our appreciation. Should arrive within [X] days.

Wishing you and your team a wonderful holiday season and a
prosperous new year!

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
```

**关键点**：
- "Nothing fancy" — 降低对方收礼的心理负担
- 不提具体礼物内容——让礼物自己说话
- 感谢是核心，礼物是载体

## Module 4: 客户谈判（Client Negotiation）

### 4.1 客户信息保护原则

**外土司铁律：客户信息是你的核心资产，绝对不能泄露给第三方**。

**保护清单**：
- 不在社交媒体上晒客户名称和订单（即使打了马赛克）
- 不在同行业交流群中分享客户信息
- 不在展会上当着其他客户的面提及客户名称
- 公司内部也不随意分享——只与直接相关的同事共享

**BYFIT 场景**：

你在广交会上，一个迪拜客户走到展位，看到你展示的样品上贴着某品牌的标签：
```
❌ "Oh, we also supply [Brand Name] in the UAE!" （泄露客户信息）

✅ "We work with several brands in the Middle East. I can share
   general references without disclosing specific names — I'm sure
   you understand the importance of confidentiality."
```

### 4.2 报价错误的补救技巧

**原则：报价错了不要慌，有策略地纠正**。

**场景：BYFIT报价时把$14.80打成了$1.48**

**Step 1: 及时发现，尽快纠正**

```
Subject: Correction — BYFIT quotation [Reference Number]

Hi [Name],

I need to correct a typo in my quotation sent earlier today.

The price for Rubber Tile 600x600x15mm should be $14.80/sqm FOB
(not $1.48/sqm). I apologize for the error.

All other items in the quotation remain unchanged.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**注意**：
- 不要假装没发现，等客户指出更尴尬
- 不要找借口（"系统错误"），直接承认打错了
- 纠正时只改错误项，不要趁机改其他项

**Step 2: 如果客户已经接受了低价**

```
Hi [Name],

I appreciate your quick response. Unfortunately, the $1.48 price
was a typographical error — the correct price is $14.80/sqm.

I understand this is a significant difference and I take full
responsibility for the error. As a gesture of goodwill, I can
offer you a 3% discount on the corrected price ($14.36/sqm)
for your first order.

I hope you'll understand — honest mistakes happen, and I'd rather
be transparent than have this cause issues later.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### 4.3 处理不合理要求

**外土司原则：不合理要求不是"不答应"，而是"用条件换条件"或"用专业说服"**。

**场景1：客户要求1个起订**

```
❌ "Our MOQ is 500 sqm, sorry."
✅ "We can accommodate smaller quantities through our sample program.
   For orders under 500 sqm, the unit price would be slightly higher
   due to production setup costs. But it's a great way to test the
   market before committing to a full container. Shall I send you
   the small-order pricing?"
```

**场景2：客户要求100%赊销**

```
❌ "We don't offer credit terms for new customers."
✅ "I understand your concern about prepayment. Here's a middle-ground:
   We can start with 50% deposit + 50% before shipment. After 3
   successful orders with on-time payments, we can discuss more
   flexible terms. This builds trust gradually — which benefits
   both sides in the long run."
```

**场景3：客户要求你承担全部运费**

```
❌ "We only do FOB terms."
✅ "I can offer CIF terms which include freight to your port. However,
   the freight cost would be built into the unit price. Alternatively,
   FOB terms give you more control over shipping and may save you 5-8%
   on logistics. Would you like me to quote both ways so you can
   compare?"
```

## Module 5: 收款方法（Payment Collection）

### 5.1 九步催款法 — 从情感诉求到逐步升级

外土司的催款哲学：**催款不是讨债，是维护正常商业秩序。但要有节奏，先软后硬**。

| 步骤 | 方法 | 语气 | 时间点 |
|------|------|------|--------|
| 1 | 友好提醒 | 温和 | 到期日+3天 |
| 2 | 确认是否遗漏 | 委婉 | 到期日+7天 |
| 3 | 提供便利 | 帮助 | 到期日+14天 |
| 4 | 强调影响 | 正式 | 到期日+21天 |
| 5 | 要求明确付款计划 | 坚定 | 到期日+30天 |
| 6 | 暂停供货 | 警告 | 到期日+45天 |
| 7 | 法律通知 | 严厉 | 到期日+60天 |
| 8 | 委托催收 | 行动 | 到期日+90天 |
| 9 | 法律诉讼 | 最终手段 | 到期日+120天+ |

**BYFIT 催款邮件模板**：

**Step 1: 友好提醒**

```
Subject: Friendly reminder — Invoice #[Number] due

Hi [Name],

Hope you're doing well. Just a quick note that Invoice #[Number]
for $[Amount] was due on [Date]. Perhaps it slipped through the
cracks — no worries at all.

Could you kindly arrange payment at your earliest convenience?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Step 3: 提供便利**

```
Subject: Payment options for Invoice #[Number]

Hi [Name],

I noticed Invoice #[Number] ($[Amount]) is now 14 days past due.
I want to make this as easy as possible for you:

1. Bank transfer details are attached (same as before)
2. If a partial payment is easier right now, we can arrange that
3. If there are any issues with the invoice, please let me know
   so I can address them promptly

Just want to make sure everything is on track.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Step 5: 要求明确付款计划**

```
Subject: Payment plan request — Invoice #[Number] — 30 days past due

Dear [Name],

Invoice #[Number] for $[Amount] is now 30 days past due.

Given the outstanding duration, I need to request a clear payment
commitment:

Option A: Full payment by [Date — 7 days from now]
Option B: Payment plan — 50% by [Date] + 50% by [Date]

Please confirm your preferred option by [Date — 3 days from now].

I value our partnership and want to resolve this amicably.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

**Step 6: 暂停供货**

```
Subject: Important — Shipment hold notice

Dear [Name],

Regrettably, we must place a hold on all pending shipments to
[Company Name] until Invoice #[Number] ($[Amount], 45 days past
due) is resolved.

This includes:
- Order #[Number] (currently in production)
- Order #[Number] (ready for shipment)

We take no pleasure in this action. Once payment is received,
we will immediately release all shipments and restore normal
service.

Please contact me urgently to discuss resolution.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
BYFIT | Finance Department
```

### 5.2 催款预防措施

**比催款更重要的是预防**：

1. **新客户**：坚持30%+70%付款条件，不做100%赊销
2. **大额订单**：分批付款——每批出货付一批
3. **高风险地区**：要求L/C而非T/T
4. **建立信用档案**：记录每个客户的付款习惯
5. **设置付款里程碑**：在PI中明确付款日期和逾期条款

## Module 6: 展会跟进（Trade Show Follow-up）

### 6.1 五邮件展会跟进序列

外土司的展会跟进铁律：**当天晚上必须发第一封邮件，展会记忆不超过48小时**。

| 邮件 | 时间 | 角度 | 目的 |
|------|------|------|------|
| S1 | 当天晚上 | 个性化回忆 | 唤起对方对你的记忆 |
| S2 | Day 2 | 详细资料 | 补充展位上没来得及讲的内容 |
| S3 | Day 5 | 报价方案 | 从信息分享进入商务讨论 |
| S4 | Day 10 | 案例佐证 | 用同类客户案例降低风险感知 |
| S5 | Day 15 | 行动推进 | 推进到样品或通话 |

**S1: 当晚第一封邮件**

```
Subject: Great meeting you at [Show Name] — BYFIT booth #[Number]

Hi [Name],

It was a pleasure meeting you at [Show Name] today. I really
enjoyed our conversation about [specific topic you discussed —
e.g., "the growing demand for EPDM color tiles in the Turkish
market"].

As promised, I'll send you more details tomorrow. In the
meantime, here's a quick photo of the sample you were interested
in:

[Photo of specific product they touched/asked about at the booth]

Safe travels back to [Country]!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**关键点**：
- 必须提到具体对话内容——证明你记得他，不是群发
- 照片是强记忆锚点——让对方看到照片就想起你
- "Safe travels" — 展会期间的人文关怀，非常加分

**S2: 详细资料邮件**

```
Subject: Following up — BYFIT rubber flooring details as discussed

Hi [Name],

As promised, here are the details on the products we discussed:

1. Rubber Tiles — Interlocking System
   - 600x600mm, available in 8mm/10mm/15mm/20mm
   - SBR (black) and SBR+EPDM (color fleck) options
   - Full spec sheet attached

2. Pilates Reformer — Studio Pro Model
   - Maple wood frame, 5-spring system
   - Foldable design for space-saving
   - Detailed catalog attached

3. For your market in [Country], based on our discussion, I'd
   recommend starting with [specific recommendation]

Shall I prepare a quotation for the items you flagged?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**S3-S5**: 参考毅冰的报价邮件组和跟进邮件组逻辑，结构类似。

### 6.2 展会跟进关键原则

1. **48小时法则**：超过48小时再联系，对方已经忘了你
2. **个性化**：每封邮件必须包含展会上的具体对话点——"群发感"是致命的
3. **分级处理**：A级客户当晚电话/邮件，B级当晚邮件，C级次日邮件
4. **不要发catalog**：展会上给过纸质catalog了，邮件里发catalog只会被忽略——发具体的产品信息和专业建议
5. **拍照存档**：展位上与客户合影（征得同意），后续邮件中附照片增强记忆

## Module 7: LinkedIn优化

### 7.1 Headline — 不是职位描述，是问题解决者

**外土司铁律：LinkedIn Headline 不是让你写"Sales Manager"，而是让客户一眼看出你能帮他解决什么问题**。

**错误 vs 正确**：

```
❌ Sales Manager at BYFIT
❌ Export Director | Gym Equipment | 10 Years Experience
✅ Helping gym distributors reduce flooring return rates by 90%
   | Virgin SBR & EPDM rubber flooring | BYFIT
✅ Your gym equipment supply partner — from sourcing to after-sales
   | BYFIT | OEM/ODM available
✅ Pilates equipment for studios & distributors — foldable reformers,
   competitive pricing | BYFIT
```

**Headline 公式**：

```
[你帮客户解决的问题] | [你的产品/服务] | [公司名]
```

### 7.2 内容发布策略

**频率**：每周2次（周二 + 周四最佳）

**内容类型及比例**：

| 类型 | 比例 | BYFIT 示例 |
|------|------|-----------|
| 行业洞察 | 40% | "Why rubber flooring density matters more than thickness — a technical deep-dive" |
| 项目案例 | 25% | "We just completed a 2,000 sqm gym flooring project in Dubai. Here are 3 lessons learned." |
| 产品知识 | 20% | "EPDM vs SBR rubber flooring: which one for which application?" |
| 个人故事 | 15% | "My visit to a gym in [City] taught me more about flooring requirements than any spec sheet." |

**发布最佳时间**：
- 美国受众：北京时间周二/周四 22:00-23:00（美东上午10-11点）
- 欧洲受众：北京时间周二/周四 16:00-17:00（欧洲上午9-10点）
- 全球混合：北京时间周二/周四 18:00（兼顾欧美的中间时间）

### 7.3 DM沟通法则

**外土司三不原则**：
1. **第一次DM绝不推销** — 先建立连接，再谈业务
2. **不群发模板** — 每条DM必须个性化
3. **不催促** — LinkedIn是关系平台，不是即时通讯

**LinkedIn DM 序列**：

**DM 1: 连接请求**

```

connect and share insights on the gym equipment market.

[Your Name]

```

```
relevant given your focus area.

[Link to article]

No strings attached — just sharing something useful.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**DM 3: 业务探索（DM2后1-2周，如果对方有互动）**

```
Hi [Name],

Glad you found the article helpful! I'm curious — what's the
biggest challenge you're facing with gym flooring supply right now?

We manufacture rubber flooring and pilates equipment at BYFIT,
and I'm always looking to understand distributor pain points
better.

If it makes sense, I'd be happy to explore how we might work
together.

Best,
[Your Name]
```
