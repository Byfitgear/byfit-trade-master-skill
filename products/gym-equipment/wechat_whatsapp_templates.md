## WhatsApp Quick Replies — 5 Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Quick Product Intro After Inquiry

**Trigger**: Customer sends a WhatsApp message asking about gym equipment or responds to an ad

```
Hi [Name]! 👋 Eric here from BYFIT — thanks for reaching out about gym equipment.

We're a manufacturer in Shandong, China with 20 production lines. Here's the quick version:

🏋️ STRENGTH: Pin-loaded, plate-loaded, multi-stations (4-10 stations)
🏃 CARDIO: Treadmills, ellipticals, spin bikes, rowing machines
🔗 FUNCTIONAL: Cable crossovers, functional trainers, power racks
⚖️ FREE WEIGHTS: Dumbbells, plates, kettlebells, bars

Key specs: 3mm steel frames | 45kg/m³ foam | 1,800lb cables | CE + ISO + SGS certified
FOB Qingdao | MOQ 5-10 units | Lead time 20-25 days

What are you looking for specifically? I'll send you the right catalog + pricing. 📋
```

**Tips**:
- Keep under 300 words — WhatsApp is for quick scanning
- Use emojis sparingly but strategically (visual anchors for each category)
- End with a question that requires a specific answer, not "let me know"
- Attach 1-2 product photos of your best-selling items if available

### Scenario 2: Price Quote Follow-Up

**Trigger**: Customer received a quotation but hasn't responded in 2-3 days

```
Hi [Name], just checking if you had a chance to review the quotation I sent on [day]? 📊

A few things that might help your decision:

✅ The prices are valid for [X] more days
✅ We can adjust the product mix to fit your budget
✅ Custom color + logo included at no extra cost for orders of 10+ units

Also — if you're comparing with other suppliers, I'm happy to do a spec comparison. Just send me their specs and I'll show you where BYFIT delivers more value per dollar. 💪

What's your timeline for this order?
```

**Tips**:
- Reference the specific date you sent the quote
- Offer concrete next steps, not just "any questions?"
- The spec comparison offer is a powerful differentiator — most suppliers avoid direct comparison
- Keep tone helpful, not pushy

1. I'll send the PI (Proforma Invoice) now
2. You confirm and arrange payment
3. We start production tomorrow
4. I'll send you production photos + testing video before shipment

```

**Tips**:
- Include the refund policy — it reduces perceived risk
- Give specific next steps in numbered format
- Mention production photos — builds trust during the wait
- Confirm shipping destination to show you're thinking ahead

**Trigger**: Customer's order is in production — proactive update at key milestones

**Version A — Frame welding complete (Day 5-7)**:

✅ Frame welding complete — all [X] units
📸 Here are photos from the production line [attach 2-3 photos]

Next: Surface treatment (powder coating in [color])
Then: Assembly + QC

Everything is on schedule for shipment by [date]. I'll update you again after coating is done. 👍
```

**Version B — QC passed, ready to ship (Day 20-25)**:
```
Hi [Name], your order is ready! 🎉

✅ All [X] units passed QC inspection
✅ Packed and waiting for container loading
📦 Container loading scheduled: [date]
🚢 ETD Qingdao: [date]
📍 ETA [destination port]: [date]

Action needed: Please arrange the 70% balance payment so we can load on schedule. Bank details on the PI I sent earlier.

I'll send you the loading photos + B/L copy once the container sails. 🚢
```

**Tips**:
- Proactive updates eliminate the customer's anxiety about "what's happening with my order"
- Photos are the most powerful trust-building tool on WhatsApp — always include them
- Give the customer a specific action item when needed (balance payment)
- Keep updates factual and milestone-based

### Scenario 5: Post-Delivery Check-In

**Trigger**: 7-14 days after the customer receives the shipment

```
Hi [Name]! Hope your BYFIT equipment arrived safely. 📦

Quick check-in:
1️⃣ Did all units arrive in good condition?
2️⃣ Have you started assembling? Any questions?
3️⃣ How's the cable smoothness and pad quality?

We want to make sure everything meets your expectations. If there are any issues — even small ones — let me know right away and we'll sort it out. 🔧

Also, I've attached our assembly video links for [models] in case your team needs them. And remember, your spare parts kit and extra cables are included in the shipment — check the accessories box.

Looking forward to your feedback! 🙏
```

**Tips**:
- Send this proactively — don't wait for the customer to report problems
- Specific questions get better responses than "how is everything?"
- Attach assembly resources — saves them from searching
- This is also a natural time to mention repeat orders: "If the quality meets your expectations, I'd love to discuss your next order"

## Message Timing Best Practices

| Channel | Best Response Time | Best Send Time (Client Local) | Max Messages/Day |
|---------|-------------------|-------------------------------|------------------|
| WhatsApp | Within 2 hours during business hours | 9:00-11:00 AM, 2:00-4:00 PM | 3-4 (avoid spam) |
| WeChat | Within 1 hour | 10:00-11:30 AM, 8:00-9:30 PM | 2-3 (less is more) |

## File Sharing Guidelines

| File Type | WhatsApp | WeChat |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| PDF catalog | Under 100MB, use Google Drive link for larger | Under 20MB recommended |
| Photos | Send 3-5 max per message, compress to 1-2MB each | 9-image grid max |
| Videos | Under 16MB (60-90 sec), use YouTube/TikTok link for longer | Under 25MB, or use video account |
| Quotation | PDF preferred, also send Excel for editable version | PDF + screenshot preview |
