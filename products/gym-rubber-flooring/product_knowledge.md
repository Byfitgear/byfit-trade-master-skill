## Materials Breakdown

### Virgin Rubber
- Highest purity, no recycled content
- Best elasticity and odor-free
- Used in premium rolls and top layers
- Higher cost, premium positioning

### Recycled SBR (Styrene-Butadiene Rubber)
- Made from recycled truck tires
- Cost-effective, eco-friendly story
- Core material for most products
- Slight rubber odor (dissipates in 1–2 weeks with ventilation)
- Density: 800–1200 kg/m³

### EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) Granule
- UV resistant, color fast
- Used for colored top surfaces
- Premium positioning
- Non-toxic, suitable for children's areas
- Available in 20+ standard colors

### Material Selection Guide

| Use Case | Recommended Material | Reason |
|----------|---------------------|--------|
| Budget commercial gym | Recycled SBR | Best value, proven durability |
| Premium gym / studio | EPDM color top | Aesthetic + performance |
| Home gym | Recycled SBR 15–20mm | Cost-effective, easy install |
| Kids' area | EPDM color top (non-toxic) | Safety, bright colors, non-toxic |
| Outdoor gym | EPDM top (UV resistant) | Weather and UV resistance |
| Horse stall / heavy duty | High-density SBR | Maximum durability |

## Quality Control Process

```
Raw Material Inspection → Mixing Ratio Check → Vulcanization Temperature Monitoring
→ Thickness Measurement → Density Testing → Visual Inspection (100%)
→ Dimensional Check → Hardness Test → Packaging Verification
→ Random Batch Testing (AQL 2.5)
```

**Quality Metrics:**
- Thickness tolerance: ±0.5mm
- Dimension tolerance: ±2mm
- Hardness (Shore A): 60–70°
- Density: 900–1,200 kg/m³ (depending on specification)
- Tensile strength: ≥2.5 MPa
- Elongation at break: ≥80%
- Abrasion resistance: ≤2.5 cm³/1.61km
