## Distribution Structure (Typical Margin Chain)

```
Factory (BYFIT) → Importer → Distributor → Retailer → End User

Margin Breakdown:
- Factory: Sells at FOB price (baseline)
- Importer: +20–30% margin (covers freight, duty, customs, warehousing)
- Distributor: +15–25% margin (covers regional logistics, sales team, stock)
- Retailer: +30–50% margin (covers showroom, marketing, retail overhead)
- End User: Pays full retail price (2.5–4x FOB price)

Example for 20mm Interlocking Tile:
- BYFIT FOB: $6.00/sqm
- Importer Landed: $9.00/sqm (+30% after freight/duty)
- Distributor: $11.25/sqm (+25%)
- Retailer: $16.90/sqm (+50%)
- End User: $16.90/sqm

BYFIT's FOB price = 35–40% of end-user retail price
```

## Market Trends

### 1. Eco-Friendly & Recycled Materials
- Growing demand for products with recycled content
- EPD (Environmental Product Declaration) becoming requested
- Circular economy regulations emerging in EU
- **BYFIT Advantage:** Recycled SBR content is already a core material — emphasize eco-story

### 2. Customization & Branding
- Gyms want branded flooring (logo, colors matching brand identity)
- Boutique fitness studios driving demand for unique aesthetics
- **BYFIT Advantage:** Custom logo embossing, Pantone color matching, EPDM color tiles

### 3. Anti-Microbial & Hygiene
- Post-COVID, anti-microbial flooring demand surged
- Hospitals, schools, and gyms seeking hygienic flooring
- **BYFIT Opportunity:** Develop anti-microbial additive option; market as premium feature

### 4. Acoustic Properties
- Noise complaints in mixed-use buildings driving demand
- Acoustic underlayment as a selling point
- **BYFIT Advantage:** Rubber's natural sound absorption; can provide acoustic test data

### 5. Digital/Smart Flooring
- Emerging trend: embedded sensors for performance tracking
- Long-term opportunity for ODM partnerships
- **BYFIT Opportunity:** Position for future smart flooring partnerships

### 6. Home Gym Permanence
- Post-COVID home gym market remains elevated vs. pre-pandemic
- DIY installation (interlocking tiles) remains popular
- **BYFIT Advantage:** Interlocking tiles are easiest for home installation
