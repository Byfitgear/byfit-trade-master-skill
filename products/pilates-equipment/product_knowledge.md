## Materials Breakdown

### Maple Wood
- Hardwood — high structural strength, resists warping
- Natural aesthetic — premium look and feel
- Used for reformer frames, chair frames, barrel frames
- Available in natural maple, walnut stain, and custom finishes
- Moisture content controlled at 8–12% to prevent cracking

### Aluminum Alloy
- 6061-T6 grade — lightweight, corrosion-resistant
- Used for reformer carriage tracks, footbar supports, structural reinforcements
- Anodized surface finish — scratch and wear resistant
- Combines with maple wood for "warm wood + cool metal" premium aesthetic

### Chrome-Plated Steel
- Q235 steel core + chrome plating
- Used for Cadillac frames, tower frames, ladder barrel rungs
- Anti-rust, anti-corrosion — suitable for humid studio environments
- Hospital-grade hygiene — easy to sanitize
- Load-bearing capacity exceeds 200 kg

### Precision Springs
- Swedish music wire (high-carbon steel, per ISO 8458)
- Tension levels: Light (Yellow, ~2.5 kg), Medium (Green, ~4.5 kg), Heavy (Red, ~6.5 kg)
- Calibration consistency: ±5% tension variance (industry standard: ±15%)
- Spring lifespan: 20,000+ compression cycles before tension loss
- Color-coded for easy identification

### Upholstery
- **PU Leather** (standard): Durable, easy to clean, 1.2mm thickness
- **PVC Leather** (economy): Water-resistant, cost-effective, 1.0mm thickness
- **Genuine Leather** (premium): Available on request, +30% cost
- Foam density: 40–50 kg/m³ (high-density, retains shape over years)
- Color options: Black (standard), Grey, Navy, White, Custom (Pantone matched)
- Anti-bacterial treatment available (+5% cost)

### Hardware & Fasteners
- All bolts, screws, and connectors: 304 stainless steel
- Carriage wheels: Polyurethane (PU) — silent, wear-resistant
- Rope/cord: 6mm braided nylon — rated to 200 kg breaking strength
- Footbar: Multi-layer birch + rubber grip surface

## Quality Control Process

```
Raw Material Inspection (wood moisture, steel grade, spring tension)
→ Frame Welding/Joining Quality Check
→ Surface Treatment Inspection (chrome plating, wood finish)
→ Carriage Assembly & Glide Test (lateral force deviation <0.5N)
→ Spring Tension Calibration Test (±5% verification)
→ Upholstery Inspection (stitching, padding, color)
→ Full Assembly Test (all moving parts, adjustments)
→ Weight Load Test (1.5x rated capacity)
→ Packaging Verification
→ Random Batch Testing (AQL 2.5)
```

**Quality Metrics:**
- Frame dimensional tolerance: ±2mm
- Carriage glide force: <3N (horizontal)
- Lateral wobble: <0.5mm (measured at carriage edge)
- Spring tension consistency: ±5% of rated value
- Upholstery stitch density: ≥8 stitches per 30mm
- Wood moisture content: 8–12%
- Chrome plating thickness: ≥15μm
- Weight capacity safety factor: 1.5x rated load
