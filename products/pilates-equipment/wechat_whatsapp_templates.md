## Quick Reference: WhatsApp vs. WeChat Usage Guide

| Aspect | WhatsApp | WeChat |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Primary Markets** | US, UK, EU, Middle East, Australia | China-based contacts, some SE Asia |
| **Tone** | Professional but casual | Warm, relationship-focused |
| **Response Speed** | Fast — real-time chat expected | Fast — but longer messages acceptable |
| **Content Format** | Short messages + images + voice notes | Longer messages + articles + mini-programs |
| **Best For** | Quick questions, order updates, price discussions | Relationship building, catalog sharing, festival greetings |
| **Avoid** | Long paragraphs — break into bullet points | Overly formal language — keep it conversational |
| **CTA Style** | Direct: "Shall I send the quote?" | Softer: "方便的话可以告诉我..." |
| **Follow-up Frequency** | Every 2-3 days | Every 3-5 days |
| **Media Sharing** | Product photos, short videos, PDF catalogs | Product photos, articles, WeChat files |
