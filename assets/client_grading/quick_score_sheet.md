### Scoring Guide per Dimension

#### 1. Purchase Intent (0-25)

| Score | Signal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **20-25** | Named specific product + quantity + timeline | "I need 500m² of 15mm rubber tiles for a gym project, delivery by March" |
| **10-19** | Named product category, no specifics yet | "I'm interested in your rubber flooring line" |
| **0-9** | Generic or no product interest | "Send me your catalog" / just browsing at booth |

#### 2. Company Quality (0-20)

| Score | Signal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **16-20** | Domain email + verified website + LinkedIn company page | sales@realcompany.com, professional website |
| **8-15** | Domain email but limited online presence | company-specific email but no LinkedIn or sparse website |
| **0-7** | Free email (Gmail/Yahoo) or no company name | johndoe@gmail.com, no company mentioned |

#### 3. Volume Potential (0-20)

| Score | Signal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **16-20** | Container-level discussion | "We order 3-5 containers per quarter" |
| **8-15** | Mixed — some products in volume, others samples | "We'd like a sample of the reformer, but we buy rubber tiles by the container" |
| **0-7** | Sample-only or unclear volume | "Can I get a sample?" with no volume context |

#### 4. Market Fit (0-20)

| Score | Signal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **16-20** | Perfect match — they sell exactly what we make to the same end-users | Gym equipment distributor in Australia selling to commercial gyms |
| **8-15** | Good match — overlapping but not identical | Sporting goods retailer who carries some gym equipment |
| **0-7** | Weak match — stretch or unclear fit | General importer with no gym/fitness focus |

#### 5. Communication Quality (0-15)

| Score | Signal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **12-15** | Fast reply (<24h), professional, detailed, asks good questions | Responds within hours with specific technical questions |
| **6-11** | Reasonable reply time (1-3 days), somewhat detailed | Replies within a day or two, gives some information |
| **0-5** | Slow (>3 days), vague, one-word answers, or no reply | Takes a week to reply with "send price" |

#### 6. Social Signals (0-10)

| Score | Signal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **8-10** | Active LinkedIn (posts monthly), hiring, recent news coverage | Company just posted about expansion, hiring sales reps |
| **4-7** | LinkedIn exists but not very active, some company info | Company page exists, last post 6+ months ago |
| **0-3** | No LinkedIn, no social presence, no recent news | Cannot find company on any social platform |

#### 7. Purchasing Match (0-10)

| Score | Signal | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **8-10** | Import history from China + buying cycle matches our production | Panjiva/shipping data shows regular imports from Shandong/Qingdao |
| **4-7** | Some import history but infrequent or from other regions | Imports from China but not Shandong, or imports are sporadic |
| **0-3** | No visible import history or new to importing | First-time importer or no data found |

## Red Flags — Auto-Downgrade

> If ANY of these appear, subtract 15 points from total score (minimum score = 0).

| # | Red Flag | Why It's a Red Flag |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Gmail/Yahoo email + no company name** | Likely not a real business buyer |
| 2 | **"Send me your full catalog" with no specifics** | Catalog collectors, not buyers. Wastes time and gives away pricing. |
| 3 | **Refuses to share company details** | If they won't tell you who they are, they're not ready to buy |
| 4 | **Multiple price-only questions with no product discussion** | Price shoppers, not value buyers. Will leave for $0.01 cheaper. |
| 5 | **Asks for exclusivity on first contact** | Unreasonable demand before any relationship = red flag |
| 6 | **Wants free samples shipped express at your cost** | Sample abuser. Real buyers understand sample terms. |
| 7 | **"We can pay 50% now, 50% in 6 months"** | Payment terms that signal cash flow problems |
| 8 | **No website + no LinkedIn + no business registration** | Can't verify the company exists |

---

## Green Flags — Auto-Upgrade

> If ANY of these appear, add 10 points to total score (maximum score = 120).

| # | Green Flag | Why It's a Green Flag |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Company domain email + detailed product specs in inquiry** | Real buyer who knows what they want |
| 2 | **Mentions current supplier issues** | Pain = motivation. They need a replacement and are actively looking. |
| 3 | **Asks about certifications** | Indicates they're serious about compliance — a real commercial buyer. |
| 4 | **Requests samples with a clear timeline** | "I need samples by [date] for a client presentation" = real project |
| 5 | **Has visited your booth at a trade show** | Already invested time in face-to-face = higher intent than cold leads |
| 6 | **Provides project details upfront** | "We're building a 2000m² gym in [city], need flooring by [date]" = real deal |
| 7 | **Asks about OEM/customization** | Brand-building intent = long-term partnership potential |
| 8 | **References your competitors by name** | They're market-educated and comparing options — you're on the shortlist. |

## Product-Specific Quick Assessment Questions

> Use these to fast-track scoring when the client names a specific product line.

### Rubber Flooring — Quick Assessment

| Question | High-Intent Answer | Low-Intent Answer |
|---|---|---|
| What thickness are you looking for? | "15mm or 20mm for commercial gym use" | "Not sure, what do you have?" |
| What's the installation type? | "Interlock for DIY, or glue-down for permanent" | "Whatever is easier" |
| Do you need slip rating certification? | "Yes, R10+ minimum for [market]" | "What's a slip rating?" |
| What's the project size? | "2000m² for a gym chain, 3 locations" | "Maybe a small order first" |

### Gym Equipment — Quick Assessment

| Question | High-Intent Answer | Low-Intent Answer |
|---|---|---|
| What type of equipment? | "Power racks, benches, cable stations for commercial use" | "Gym stuff" |
| Steel grade requirement? | "Q345B minimum for commercial warranty" | "What's Q345B?" |
| Are you OEM or standard? | "We want our logo on the frames" | "Whatever you have" |
| Container planning? | "We need a mix of racks and benches, about 2x 40HQ per quarter" | "Not sure about volume yet" |

### Pilates Equipment — Quick Assessment

| Question | High-Intent Answer | Low-Intent Answer |
|---|---|---|
| What pilates products? | "Reformers primarily, some Cadillacs for studio clients" | "Pilates machines" |
| Studio or home use? | "Studio-grade, 8+ hours daily use" | "Home use" |
| Customization needed? | "Yes, brand color + logo on the carriage" | "Standard is fine" |
| Volume and frequency? | "20-30 reformers per quarter, ongoing" | "Maybe 1-2 to test" |
