# Trade Show Follow-Up Templates

> **Method**: 外土司 5-email sequence — 每封邮件增加新价值，绝不说"just following up"
> **Timing Rule**: 第一封当天晚上发出（4-6小时内），后续每封间隔递增
> **Brand**: BYFIT | **Products**: Gym Rubber Flooring, Gym Equipment, Pilates Equipment | **FOB**: Qingdao
> **Contact**: Eric — sales@sdbyfit.com | **Factory**: Shandong, China | 20 Production Lines

---

## 外土司 Trade Show Follow-Up Core Principles

1. **当天必回** — 展会当晚发出第一封，趁记忆新鲜建立连接
2. **每封增值** — 不重复信息，每封提供展会未涉及的新内容
3. **具体化** — 引用展会上的具体对话、具体产品、具体需求
4. **递进式CTA** — 从"确认地址"→"看看数据"→"聊聊需求"→"定个时间"→"留个门"
5. **5封为止** — 5封后若无回复，第6封就是打扰。留门即可

### Post-Show Categorization Method — A/B/C Grading

| Grade | Criteria | Follow-Up Action | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A (Hot)** | Specific inquiry + budget + timeline + decision-maker | Full 5-email sequence + sample shipment + quote | Email 1 same evening |
| **B (Warm)** | General interest + company verification + some specificity | Full 5-email sequence + catalog + spec sheets | Email 1 within 24h |
| **C (Cool)** | Stopped by booth + took card + no specific need | Abbreviated 3-email sequence (Recap + Value Add + Graceful Close) | Email 1 within 48h |

**A-Grade Indicators:**
- Named specific product + quantity
- Asked about lead time for a real project
- Requested a quote on the spot
- Decision-maker or direct buyer (not intern/assistant)
- Company matches target market perfectly

**B-Grade Indicators:**
- Showed genuine interest but no immediate project
- Asked general questions about product range
- Has real company but unclear buying authority
- Market fit is good but timing uncertain

**C-Grade Indicators:**
- Walked by, took materials, minimal conversation
- Student or researcher, not a buyer
- Company outside target market
- No specific product interest expressed
