## 外土司 "网红式标题" Principles

1. **数字+趋势** — 人脑对数字和趋势有本能反应
2. **利益+归属** — 让读者觉得"这是说给我的"
3. **悬念+好奇** — 制造信息缺口，驱动打开
4. **反常识** — 打破预期，脱颖而出
5. **短于8词** — 手机屏幕第一行就要看到核心

## Usage Notes

1. **Never send without research** — Every template assumes you've investigated the client first (LinkedIn, website, import records, trade show data)
2. **Customize [brackets]** — Replace all bracketed content with specific, verified information
3. **One template per outreach** — Don't mix approaches. Pick the one that best fits their situation
4. **Subject line A/B test** — If sending to similar clients in batch, rotate subject variants to track open rates
5. **Follow up once** — If no reply after 5 business days, send one follow-up with new value (not "just checking in")
6. **Eric's signature** — Always include: Eric | BYFIT | [Product Line] | sales@sdbyfit.com
